Description

Sora 2 represents a groundbreaking advancement in AI video generation technology. Developed by OpenAI, this revolutionary model transforms text descriptions into stunning, high-quality videos with unprecedented realism and creativity.

With enhanced capabilities over its predecessor, Sora 2 can generate videos up to 60 seconds long, featuring complex scenes, multiple characters, and intricate camera movements. The model understands not just what you want to create, but how objects interact in the physical world.

The quality improvements in Sora 2 are remarkable - from better temporal consistency to more accurate physics simulation, every frame is crafted with attention to detail that rivals professional video production.
